About

I am Jianlong He (贺健龙). I will soon pursue my Ph.D. at the Wuhan University, where I will be advised by Prof. Chaofeng Chen. I am currently pursuing my M.Eng. degree in Computer Technology at Jiangxi University of Science and Technology, where I was advised by Prof. Huilan Luo.
